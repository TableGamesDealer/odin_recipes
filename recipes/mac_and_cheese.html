Here we see a complete dish of Mac and Cheese

Mac and Cheese is a dish that is comprised of noodles and cheese sauce.

This recipe takes alot of time to execute but is a classic of American cooking

Ingredients:

  • Pasta noodles
  • Cheese sauce
  • Milk
  • Optional meats
  • Optional garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare cheese sauce
  2. Par cook pasta noodles
  3. Admire cheese
  4. Combine cooked pasta with cheese sauce
  5. Cooked till combined
  6. Finish with butter and milk stiring to prevent breaking the sauce
  7. Let pot of pasta and sauce cool to conglomerate and gain a thick consistency
  8. Garnish with meats or herbs for flavour
  9. Enjoy with a child like awe that the universe could provide something so good
