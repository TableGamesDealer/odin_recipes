Here we see a complete dish of a quesadilla

A quesadilla is a dish comprised of tortillas and cheese.

This dish is a Mexican clasic quickly prepared of two grittled torillas with a hot and cheesy center.

Ingredients:

  • Tortilla
  • Cheese, queso preferred
  • butter or oil
  • Optional meats

Instructions:

  1. Heat up grill top or pan
  2. Heat up butter or oil in the pan
  3. Give the tortillas time to bloom on the top, you should see the tortilla blister
  4. Once the tortilla is starting to darken add cheese
  5. Optionally add topings like meat or veggies
  6. Remove from the heat and cut into triangles to serve

Quesadillas are a staple of Frontera food
