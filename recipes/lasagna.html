Here we see a complete dish of lasagna

Lasagna is a layerd dish that is baked to make a stack of goodness.

This recipe takes alot of time to execute but is a classic of Italian cooking

Ingredients:

  • Sheet pasta
  • Meat sauce
  • Cheese
  • Rendered vegetables

Instructions:

  1. Prepare meat sauce
  2. Par cook pasta sheets
  3. Render vegetables
  4. Admire cheese
  5. Layer meat sauce
  6. Layer sheet pasta
  7. Layer rendered vegetables
  8. Layer cheese
  9. Repeat layers until ingredients exauseted or panfull
  10. Cover with cheese to make a crust
  11. Bake for 30-50 minutes, untill all the goodness has conglomerated
